### Results:

**Mostly A: Foucault**\
You map systems like a haunted cartographer. You don't fight power, you *trace* it, *name* it, and let it flinch under your gaze. Your memes have citations. You\'re sexy in a terrifying way.

**Mostly B: Diogenes**\
You contain no illusions. You contain very little, actually. You see the world's performativity and refuse the play. Your barrel is real, and it\'s rent-free.

**Mostly C: Baudrillard**\
You live in the hyperreal. The map replaced the territory decades ago. You don't mourn the real; you vibe with the ghosts. Your enemies are signs, not people.

**Mostly D: Kant**\
You crave structure like oxygen. You believe in reason but you know it has limits. You\'re the friend who says "technically\..." and means it. You think ethics is real and you\'re kind of right.

**Mostly E: Deleuze (with a hint of Žižek)**\
You are chaos on a motorcycle. You love flows, rhizomes, nonsense, and the sacred joke hidden inside all serious discourse. No one understands your takes, but they *feel* true.
