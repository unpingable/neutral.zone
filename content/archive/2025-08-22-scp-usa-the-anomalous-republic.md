## Item #1776-A: SCP-USA

**Object Class:** Keter

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-USA is self-containing through a complex system of distributed participation protocols. All inhabitants within the affected area are required to engage in regular civic rituals including elections, tax filing, media consumption, and social media engagement. Attempts to exit the containment area result in \[REDACTED\] or automatic re-enrollment in participation protocols via alternative channels.

Foundation personnel embedded within SCP-USA must maintain civilian cover identities and continue normal participation in anomalous civic processes to avoid detection. Standard reality anchors have proven ineffective. Amnestics have no effect on the underlying anomaly, though they successfully erase individual memories of specific anomalous events.

**Warning:** SCP-USA treats all human subjects within its area of effect as \"citizens\" regardless of their actual Foundation classification. D-class personnel exposed to the entity report feelings of civic duty and begin voluntarily participating in anomalous electoral processes. Termination of D-class assignments within SCP-USA has proven difficult due to entity-generated \"voter registration\" and \"jury duty\" obligations.

**Note:** Attempts to exit the containment area through expatriation, \"sovereign citizen\" movements, or other withdrawal protocols are automatically absorbed as sub-routines within the entity\'s participation matrix. These apparent resistance behaviors serve to reinforce containment by providing the simulation of choice while maintaining systemic engagement.

**Description:** SCP-USA is a large-scale anomalous political entity spanning approximately 9.8 million square kilometers across North America. The anomaly presents as a democratic republic but operates according to principles that violate conventional understanding of causality, logic, and political physics.

Key anomalous properties include:

-   **Temporal Inconsistency:** SCP-USA experiences all historical periods simultaneously. Citizens regularly reference events from different decades as if they occurred concurrently. The entity maintains multiple contradictory historical narratives without apparent cognitive dissonance.

-   **Paradoxical Governance:** The entity consistently produces outcomes opposite to stated intentions while maintaining the belief that it functions as designed. Efficiency reforms generate waste, transparency measures increase opacity, and unity campaigns deepen division with mathematical precision.

-   **Reality Persistence:** SCP-USA continues operating despite meeting all conventional criteria for systemic failure multiple times. The entity appears to derive energy from crisis rather than being depleted by it, converting each apparent breakdown into fuel for continued operation.

-   **Memetic Propagation:** Exposure to SCP-USA gradually alters subjects\' understanding of normal political function. Long-term residents lose the ability to recognize the anomalous nature of their circumstances, instead interpreting increasingly bizarre events as \"unprecedented but not unexpected.\"

## Discovery

SCP-USA was first identified by Foundation researchers in \[DATA EXPUNGED\] when routine monitoring detected a political entity that had somehow achieved stable contradiction. Initial reports were dismissed as measurement error until field teams confirmed that the anomaly was not only self-sustaining but actively expanding its influence through cultural and economic channels.

The realization that Foundation personnel were themselves contained within SCP-USA led to the current research protocols, which operate under the assumption that objective analysis of the anomaly may be impossible from within its effects zone.

## Research Notes

**Dr. ███████, Lead Researcher:** \"The entity doesn\'t malfunction---it functions perfectly according to principles we don\'t understand. Traditional political science assumes linear causation between actions and outcomes. SCP-USA operates on recursive loops where effects precede causes and outcomes determine intentions retroactively.\"

**Incident Report USA-█:** During the [2024 election cycle](https://www.cnn.com/2024/11/06/politics/trump-wins-presidency/index.html), SCP-USA simultaneously experienced what appeared to be a constitutional crisis and a routine transfer of power. Citizens within the affected area perceived both events as normal, with surveys indicating 73% believed the process was \"going as expected\" while 68% described it as \"unprecedented crisis.\"

**Incident Report USA-██:** Economic indicators simultaneously reported [record-low unemployment rates](https://www.bls.gov/news.release/empsit.htm) and [widespread economic collapse](https://www.washingtonpost.com/business/2024/12/15/economy-inflation-recession-fears/). When surveyed, 81% of citizens described current economic conditions as \"about what I expected\" while 76% characterized the same conditions as \"historically unprecedented.\" No cognitive dissonance was detected in overlapping response groups.

**Update █/█/25:** Recent observations suggest SCP-USA may be preparing for some form of metamorphosis. Citizens are receiving political communications from jurisdictions they have no connection to, suggesting the entity\'s containment protocols are either failing or evolving. Research into potential breach conditions is ongoing.

## Personal Experience Reports

*Note: The following reports come from Foundation personnel stationed within SCP-USA. Their objectivity may be compromised.*

**Agent ████:** \"I\'ve been living in Pennsylvania for three years. This week I received [text messages from the California governor](https://www.latimes.com/politics/story/2025-01-15/newsom-political-action-committee-fundraising) asking for donations to influence Texas redistricting to prevent Iranian nuclear weapons. When I mentioned this to colleagues, they said it was \'weird but not surprising.\' I\'m beginning to understand why external observation of SCP-USA is considered impossible.\"

**Dr. ██████:** \"My nephew just started computer science classes despite [overwhelming evidence that the industry is collapsing](https://www.newsweek.com/computer-science-popular-college-major-has-one-highest-unemployment-rates-2076514). When I tried to explain [the economic data](https://www.latimes.com/business/story/2025-08-20/say-farewell-to-the-ai-bubble-and-get-ready-for-the-crash), he showed me recruitment videos that make the failing industry look like a heroic adventure. The entity appears to maintain multiple simultaneous realities tailored to different demographic groups.\"

**Agent █████:** \"Watched a congressional representative [call a major news network \'propaganda\'](https://www.huffpost.com/entry/marjorie-taylor-greene-propaganda-nypost-fox_n_6851834de4b025ec9a1a3aa6) while continuing to support the political figure the network promotes. Both actions occurred in the same week without apparent cognitive dissonance. The constituency interpreted this as evidence of principled independence rather than contradiction.\"

**Dr. ████████:** \"I attempted to file my taxes early using the entity\'s digital systems. The platform simultaneously accepted my payment and flagged me for noncompliance with [identical deadlines](https://www.irs.gov/filing/individuals/when-to-file). Both confirmation and violation notices arrived the same day. My designated tax professional explained this was \'normal audit lottery procedures.\' I have begun unconsciously referring to this process as \'how a democracy should function.\'\"

**Agent ███████:** \"Observed multiple municipal leaders [requesting federal protection from deportation activities](https://www.foxnews.com/us/trump-admin-deploys-2000-more-troops-blue-city-anti-ice-riots) while simultaneously receiving mandatory federal troop deployments for \'sanctuary city protection.\' Most recently documented in Boston, where ICE officials [threatened to \'flood\' the city with agents](https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/ice-chief-boston-mayor-michelle-wu-rcna226340) immediately after the mayor refused federal demands to abandon sanctuary policies. Local officials expressed gratitude for assistance with the same operations they had requested protection from. When questioned about the apparent contradiction, subjects responded that this represented \'normal federal cooperation\' and showed no signs of cognitive dissonance.\"

## Theoretical Framework

Current research suggests SCP-USA operates as what Dr. ████████ terms a \"hyperobject\"---an entity so vast and temporally distributed that contained subjects can only perceive local effects, never the entity itself. Citizens experience constant low-level crisis (political dysfunction, economic instability, social fragmentation) without recognizing these as symptoms of their containment within an anomalous system.

The entity appears to maintain stability through what researchers have termed \"retrocausal legitimacy\"---a process where each apparent crisis is absorbed and converted into evidence for the system\'s continued necessity. Traditional political collapse would provide resolution; SCP-USA prevents collapse by converting breakdown events into content for its own continuation.

## Containment Breach Scenarios

**Scenario Alpha:** Citizens begin recognizing the anomalous nature of their political system. Initial modeling suggests this would result in \[REDACTED\] or rapid evolution of the entity\'s self-containment protocols.

**Scenario Beta:** External observation of SCP-USA from outside its effects zone. Current theoretical work indicates this may be impossible due to the entity\'s influence on global information systems and economic networks.

**Scenario Gamma:** Complete breakdown of the entity\'s reality-maintenance systems. Effects unknown but likely to involve \[DATA EXPUNGED\] across multiple continents.

## Ongoing Research

The primary research challenge is that SCP-USA appears to incorporate all attempts to study it into its own operational matrix. This document itself may be serving an unknown function within the entity\'s self-maintenance protocols.

Current hypotheses under investigation:

-   Whether SCP-USA represents a single anomalous entity or a manifestation of a broader class of political hyperobjects

-   The relationship between the entity\'s temporal anomalies and its reality-persistence properties

-   Potential methods for external observation that do not result in researcher incorporation

-   The possibility that similar entities exist undetected in other regions

**Addendum:** Recent events suggest SCP-USA may be approaching some form of phase transition. Citizens report simultaneous feelings of impending collapse and absolute normality. Monitoring stations have detected unusual patterns in the entity\'s reality-distortion field, but the significance of these changes remains unclear.

Research continues under Protocol Hyperion, with the understanding that our ability to study SCP-USA may be fundamentally limited by our position within its containment area. All personnel are reminded that maintaining civilian cover identities is essential for continued observation of the anomaly.

The entity remains active and stable. Estimated containment duration: indefinite.
